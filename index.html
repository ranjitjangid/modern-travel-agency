Destinations

Jaipur, Rajasthan

Known as the "Pink City," Jaipur is famous for its historic forts, palaces, and vibrant culture, offering visitors a glimpse into Rajasthan's rich heritage.

Price : $1000

Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh

Varanasi, situated on the banks of the sacred Ganges River, is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world, revered for its spiritual significance, ancient temples, and mesmerizing evening aarti ceremonies.

Price : $1200

Munnar, Kerala

Nestled in the lush Western Ghats, Munnar is a picturesque hill station renowned for its sprawling tea plantations, tranquil valleys, and refreshing climate, making it an ideal destination for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts alike.

Price : $1500

Goa

Goa, famous for its pristine beaches, vibrant nightlife, and Portuguese heritage, is a popular tourist destination offering a perfect blend of relaxation, culture, and adventure against the backdrop of the Arabian Sea.

Price : $1200

Leh-Ladakh, Jammu and Kashmir

Leh-Ladakh, nestled in the Himalayas, is a land of breathtaking landscapes, rugged mountains, and Tibetan Buddhist monasteries, offering intrepid travelers an unforgettable experience amidst its remote and majestic surroundings.

Price : $1200
